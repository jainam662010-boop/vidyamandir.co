🪷 About This Platform

Vidya Mandir is a complete Class 10 CBSE/NCERT learning platform.
Covers Mathematics, Science, Social Science and English — all four core subjects.
Features include video lessons, chapter notes, NCERT important topics, PDF resources, interactive quizzes, and a study planner.
Use this control panel to manage all content and monitor student activity.
